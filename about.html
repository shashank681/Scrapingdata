Our Story

Born from a grandmother's recipes and a passion for authentic Indian flavors, SnaQ Trail brings the taste of home to your modern lifestyle.

From Kitchen to Your Home

Every SnaQ Trail product begins with a memory – the aroma of spices roasting in my grandmother's kitchen, the sound of crispy namkeen being prepared for festivals, and the joy of sharing these traditional treats with family and friends.

Founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to preserve the authentic taste of Indian snacks while meeting the quality standards of modern consumers. We believe that great taste shouldn't come at the cost of health, which is why every product is made with carefully selected ingredients and time-tested recipes.

Our journey started in a small kitchen with just three products. Today, we serve thousands of families across India, but our commitment to quality and authenticity remains unchanged. Every batch is prepared with the same love and attention that went into our grandmother's cooking.

Our Mission & Vision
